# External Connections Policy (Demo)

**Mapped controls:**  

- CMMC: AC.L2-3.1.20  
- NIST: AC-20  
- SOC 2: CC6.7  
- ISO: A.8.12  

**Requirements:**

- Outbound connections restricted; approved destinations only.  
- Public storage is prohibited unless waiver approved.  
- All external connections monitored through SIEM.
